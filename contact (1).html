Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How can I join the team?

Any student at Northern Secondary School can join! We welcome all skill levels. Send us a message through the form above or speak with one of our members to see how you can help.

What skills do I need?

No prior experience is required! We have roles for everyone — from engineering and manufacturing to marketing and project management.

How do sponsorships work?

We offer various sponsorship tiers with different benefits. Visit our Sponsors page for details or contact us to discuss custom arrangements.

When are meetings held?

We meet after school on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays, with optional Saturday sessions during competition season.
